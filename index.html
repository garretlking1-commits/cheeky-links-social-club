No. 01 — The Clubhouse

Every great club
has a story.

The Cheeky Links Social Club was built on a simple idea: the best part of any round isn't the scorecard — it's the walk, the company, and the unhurried hours after the eighteenth.

We grow in small batches and finish slow, the way the old members like it. Every strain is cured by hand and named for the course. Every piece in the Pro Shop is cut to be worn long after the sun goes down. No mass production. No shortcuts. Just a standard, kept.

You won't find us everywhere. That's the point. The Club is for the few who appreciate the difference — and have the good sense to play the back nine.
